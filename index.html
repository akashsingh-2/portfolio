About

About
me.

I'm Akash Singh, a Staff Product Designer based in Bengaluru — I design the enterprise software teams reach for when the work is complex and the margin for error is thin.

For over a decade I've worked inside the high-stakes corners of B2B SaaS: governance and compliance at Diligent, application delivery and networking at Citrix, physical access and identity at HID Global, and security infrastructure at Cisco and INSZoom. Different industries, one constant — dense, technical products that real businesses depend on every day.

My work tends to sit where design meets systems. I designed Diligent's Toolkit Builder from a blank canvas into a product that now underpins a $2M+ sales pipeline; I directed the Community platform's modernisation, aligning it to the existing Atlas design system; and I led the accessibility uplift from WCAG 2.1 AA all the way to 2.2 AAA. I care as much about the component that scales quietly across a hundred screens as I do about the hero flow everyone sees.

Beyond shipping, I mentor — informally coaching up to eight mid-to-senior designers — because a Staff role isn't just about your own output; it's about raising the ceiling for the people around you.

What I bring to a Staff role
Systems thinking at scale — design systems, component libraries, and accessibility standards that hold up across whole product suites.
0→1 product design — taking ambiguous, undefined problems from first sketch to shipped, revenue-generating product.
Cross-functional influence — partnering with product, engineering, and sales to move adoption and retention, not just ship screens.
Craft & mentorship — high personal quality bar, paired with lifting the designers around me.

I'm currently exploring my next Staff, Senior Staff, or Lead Product Design role — ideally somewhere the problems are hard, the users are demanding, and the stakes are worth designing carefully for.
